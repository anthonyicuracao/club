Rules of the Program

Eligibility to Purchase the Club

• Any Curacao account holder or "cash" customer is eligible to purchase any of Curacao's club memberships, as these memberships will be tied to the purchaser's name rather than an account number.

• If a Curacao account holder purchases a club membership on their Curacao account, they must be in good standing, where the account is active and transactable.

Purchasing the Club

• This program requires a 12-month commitment starting from the day of purchase, and it will auto-renew annually with a charge to the same account used for the original purchase.

• Legal team to provide language and guidelines for any documentation that will need to be signed by customers.

• After selecting a tier in the Curacao club, a one-time payment for the annual cost of the program will be added to the customer's invoice. If a Curacao account is used for the purchase, the amount will be financed according to Curacao's regular financial practices.

• When buying the club membership with the Curacao account, the club fee will not affect the available credit and will not reduce the open-to-buy.

• Existing club members can upgrade to the next tier at any time by paying the difference between the tiers. However, downgrades or cancellations will not be permitted.

• The club program benefits will become available immediately when the club fee is added to an invoice. The benefits will be reflected right away in the same transaction.

Canceling the Club

• The club program has a no-cancellation policy. However, in special cases where a full return is processed on a transaction that included the club fee in the same invoice, a full refund of the club fee will be granted, provided that none of the club benefits have been used by the member. Only Managers and CS escalation will have access to this process.

• Club members enrolled in the program for the first full year can cancel the auto-renewal process before the anniversary of their membership.

Using the Benefits as a Club Member

• Only club members will be eligible to enjoy the benefits of this program.

• Every benefit available to the club member, depending on the tier purchased, will be automatically applied to each transaction once the club membership is activated.

• The benefits of the club cannot be transferred, gifted, or used by anyone other than the individual who purchased the club membership.

• Club Discount Pricing on Specific Products: The price tags at Curacao stores and on the website will feature two prices: a lower price for club members (with a predetermined discount) and a higher price for non-club members.

• Free Shipping on Online Orders: Club members will receive free shipping on purchases made in DMA on iCuracao.com for items shipped via carriers such as FedEx, UPS, USPS, and other small parcel services. Large items requiring delivery will be excluded as they will be delivered using our regular processes.

• 0% APR Financing for 8-12 Payments: Club members will have exclusive access to short-term financing through the Curacao credit card. These transactions will be subject to credit approval and Curacao's regular financial processes. There will be no minimum purchase and all items will qualify. The program offers biweekly payments, though no changes will be made to the monthly billing cycle and financial calculations. The program encourages club members to make more frequent payments if desired.

- The short-term financing option will not be available to non-club members.

• Price Beat Guarantee: Club members will have exclusive access to Curacao's established Price Beat Guarantee (PBG) program.

- The PBG program will not be available to non-club members.

• Rewards Voucher for Every $500 Spent: Club members will receive rewards vouchers through Curacao's established process as follows:

- Tier 1: $10 for every $500 spent

- Tier 2: $20 for every $500 spent

- Tier 3: $30 for every $500 spent

- The rewards program will not be available to non-club members.

• Free Delivery: Tier 2 members will receive "free curbside delivery" (inside the home) on products requiring a truck for delivery, such as furniture, major appliances, and large TV screens that qualify under the club program. Tier 3 members will receive "white glove delivery" (inside the home with product placement) for the same types of items.
